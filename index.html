Project 3

Assignment 1

I worked independently on formstorming this week, reviewing tutorials, example videos, and additional readings from the lecture. I learned more about gesture interaction and explored the "touch" brainstorming activity, which guided my initial research where I drew from a variety of external sources. I tried to research from the perspective that touch is data, which is both proactive and reactive.

Potential concepts to research further —

Rhythm games: The Makey Makey controls immediately reminded me of rhythm games. I wanted to explore them from an interactive standpoint. For example, what makes rhythm game interactions satisfying? I could look into how visuals are generated for pattern-matching and emulate that.
Related links: Unsustainable (new media art)

Tactile art & development: This came from one of my key findings from the brainstorming activity. I came up with phrases like "holding", "grabbing", and "picking up" which reminded me of children's toys and sensory kits. I could also look into motor simulation. During this process I also looked into tactile art and sensory soothing kits. Touch has proven emotional benefits (Dachner Keltner on Touch).
This also relates back to a guided meditation website I found during project 2 ("This is a Thing"), my interest in Headspace, and how I could potentially incorporate tactile objects into mindfulness.

Experience design & machine design: I like the ideas of physical interfaces which involve dragging, moving items, and turning knobs. It might be interesting to design an unconventional controller interface. Micro keyboards have been trending (such as the Figma keypad by Work Louder) but they tend to be flat and lack texture.

Sketches overview:

  • Learned how to overlay different blending modes, use 3D features
  • Had to think about button implementation, which was more difficult than mouse movement
  • Tried to echo some of my research: e.g I did two rhythm game sketches and realized it would be easy to do a score system, I could incorporate TTS or speech synthesis into the tactile development ideas (shapes, emotions), considered machine design in the 3D zooming sketch (I wanted to create a sci-fi interface for a physical machine)

Next steps:

  • Exploring different materials, textures
  • Plan integration w/ Makey Makey
  • Approach that is more focused on design/fabrication or conceptual instead of crafting the objects from scratch (based on my personal strengths), using things and linking them into one single assembly
